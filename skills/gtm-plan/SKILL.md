## Launch Plan

### Launch Timeline

**T-8 weeks: Preparation**
- [ ] Finalize positioning & messaging
- [ ] Create marketing materials
- [ ] Set up tracking & analytics

**T-4 weeks: Internal Enablement**
- [ ] Sales team trained
- [ ] Support team trained
- [ ] Internal demo day

**T-2 weeks: Pre-Launch**
- [ ] Beta customer testimonials
- [ ] Press embargoes set
- [ ] Influencer outreach

**Launch Day (T-0)**
- [ ] Product goes live
- [ ] Announcement email sent
- [ ] Blog post published
- [ ] Social media posts
- [ ] Press release distributed

**T+1 week: Post-Launch**
- [ ] Monitor metrics
- [ ] Gather feedback
- [ ] Quick wins & optimizations

**T+4 weeks: Retrospective**
- [ ] Review results
- [ ] Document learnings
- [ ] Plan next phase
