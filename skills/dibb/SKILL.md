## DIBB Framework Overview

### What is DIBB?

The DIBB Framework is Spotify's approach to strategic product planning that emphasizes data-driven decision-making and hypothesis testing.

**Data (D):** Observable facts about our market, users, and product
**Insights (I):** Patterns and learnings we derive from analyzing the data
**Beliefs (B):** Strategic hypotheses about what will drive success
**Bets (B):** Specific initiatives we commit to testing or capitalizing on our beliefs

### DIBB Time Horizons

- **Company Beliefs:** 3-5 years (strategic direction)
- **North Star + 2-Year Goals:** Ambitious, measurable targets
- **Company Bets:** 6-12 months (large cross-organizational projects)
- **Product/Team Bets:** Quarterly (tactical initiatives)

### Success Criteria

DIBBs are validated when:
- Data supports the belief
- Insights reveal actionable opportunities
- Bets show measurable progress toward belief validation
- Learnings inform next iteration of beliefs
